1) Class 2 equipment is safer than Class 1, it does not rely on earth protection.

2) It is protected by at least two layers from insulation.

3) It is double insulated equipment.

4) It is marked with a double square symbol. (Square inside square)

5) Examples: computers, power tools, lamps, photocopiers.

6) It doesn't require a full PAT test. It needs insulation tests, a visual inspection and users check.
