Electiral shock and its grades.

Electiral shock - If electric current passes a human body, some physiological effects can happen, so it is very harmful for important organs and systems. Affects can be changed according to its nature:

I grade -> The person does not lose consciousness, some systemic disorders can happen.

II grade -> The person lose consciousness, but heart and breathing does not stop.

III grade -> The person lose consciousness, there is some problems in breathing and heart.

IV grade -> Heart stops working, breathing stops. It cause clinical death.
