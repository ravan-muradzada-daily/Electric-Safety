Terminalogy:

1. Traumatic shock - If strong irritation happens, a neuro-reflex reaction happends in the body.

2. Terminal condition - It goes in several levels: agony(pain), clinical death and biological death

3. Clinical death - Breating and circulation stop, the pupil of the eyes are wide open, do not react a light. Even painful external influences does not cause reaction.

4. Organic - it happens as a disorder or stopping of biological processes.

5. Electrolyte - expressed in decomposition of organic fluids which results in a change in their chemical properties.

6. Thermal - causes heating or burns on external or internal organs.

7. Mechanical - It can happen on nerves, blood vessels and tissues. Unexpected narrowing of muscles parts.
